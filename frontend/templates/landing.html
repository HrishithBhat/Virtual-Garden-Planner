Project overview

AI‑Garden is a complete gardening companion. Manage plant collections, build personalized care schedules, get instant disease insights from leaf photos, and discover curated tools and supplies in the marketplace. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned grower, AI‑Garden helps you keep plants thriving with simple, actionable guidance.

What you can do

01

Smart plant management

Browse plants with photos and details, then add them to your personal garden in one click.

02

Personalized schedules

Generate day‑by‑day watering and care schedules, track tasks, and get reminders.

03

AI disease detection

Upload a leaf photo to get instant, AI‑assisted diagnosis and practical care steps.

04

AI assistant chat

Ask questions anytime — fertilizing, pests, soil, lighting — get concise, friendly tips.

05

Marketplace

Find tools, seeds, and supplies matched to your plants with quick links to buy.

06

Admin & roles

Admin panel plus sub‑admins for plants, ML training, and marketplace management.

AI‑Garden details

  • Disease detection uses local ML when available; otherwise falls back to a cloud AI for safe, concise guidance.
  • Conversations are context‑aware: the assistant tailors tips to the plants in your garden.
  • Schedules are stored and tasks can be checked off; daily reminders surface on your dashboard.
  • Your data is organized per‑user with role‑based access for collaborative management.
